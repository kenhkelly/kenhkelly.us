Hey all. Just a quick tip I discovered when playing out with Trello.

In a textarea of Firefox, typically when you hit tab, it moves to the next element. After mashing a few key combos, I discovered that you can put a tab character in the text box with Option Shift Tab on Mac. I haven’t tested this in Windows, or other browsers though. Let me know if it’s the same equivalent.
