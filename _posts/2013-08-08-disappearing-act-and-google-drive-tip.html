For my return back, here is a nice little Google Drive tip that I found on funbutlearn.com. You can use Google Drive to host and download files directly. You do that by plugging the file’s unique key into a direct download link.

You can find your key when you open the file in the default view:

https://docs.google.com/file/d/<KEY>/edit

Then you can take that key and plug it into this url:

http://drive.google.com/uc?id=<KEY>&e=download

Voila! You can also hotlink images from Google Drive into your site.
