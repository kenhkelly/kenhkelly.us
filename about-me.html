About Me

In my younger years, I was fascinated with web technology, and as a result I built my first website, a fan-site for a video game I played at the time. From that day forward, I was hooked.

After many years of dedication and hard-work, I worked to perfect my craft, exceptional web design, front-end design, and superior back-end development. Coupled with speed and power, my websites are modern, clean, and functional.

To accomplish good looking web presences, I have had the opportunity to learn graphic design using Photoshop, Illustrator, and motion graphics using After Effects.

My passion is my career path, and it shows in my work. I am proud of everything that I have learned and accomplished. I am very excited to see where this new web technology will take us.
